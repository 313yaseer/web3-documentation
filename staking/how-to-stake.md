# How to Stake Tokens

Earn rewards by staking your tokens to support network security.

## Prerequisites

- Connected wallet (MetaMask recommended)
- Minimum stake amount: 100 tokens
- Sufficient ETH for gas fees (~0.01 ETH)

## Step-by-Step Staking Guide

### Step 1: Navigate to Staking Page

1. Go to our platform
2. Click "Staking" in navigation menu
3. Ensure wallet is connected

### Step 2: Choose Stake Amount

1. Enter amount to stake (minimum: 100 tokens)
2. Review staking terms:
   - **Lock period**: 30 days
   - **APY**: ~12% (variable)
   - **Rewards distribution**: Weekly
3. Click "Approve" (first-time only)

### Step 3: Approve Token Spending

1. MetaMask popup appears
2. Review approval transaction
3. Click "Confirm"
4. Wait for confirmation (~30 seconds)

### Step 4: Confirm Staking Transaction

1. After approval, click "Stake"
2. MetaMask popup appears again
3. Review staking details:
   - Amount
   - Gas fee
   - Total cost
4. Click "Confirm"
5. Wait for confirmation (~1-2 minutes)

### Step 5: Verify Staking

You should see:
- ✅ Staked balance updated
- ✅ Estimated rewards
- ✅ Unlock date
- ✅ Transaction hash

## Staking Rewards

**How rewards work:**
- **Distribution**: Every 7 days (Mondays at 00:00 UTC)
- **Auto-compound**: Rewards automatically restake
- **Claiming**: Can claim manually anytime

**Reward calculation:**
```
Daily Reward = (Your Stake / Total Staked) × Daily Emission × APY
```

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Lock Period**: Tokens are locked for 30 days from stake date
⚠️ **Early Unstaking**: 10% penalty if unstaked before lock period
⚠️ **Gas Fees**: Required for staking, unstaking, and claiming

## Risks

1. **Smart Contract Risk**: Contracts are audited but not risk-free
2. **Impermanent Loss**: Not applicable (single-asset staking)
3. **Lock Period**: Tokens inaccessible during lock
4. **APY Variability**: Rates may change based on total staked
